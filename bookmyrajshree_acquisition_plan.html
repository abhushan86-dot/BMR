Section 4
Android APK Funnel — Deep Dive
The Android APK funnel is the most critical — and most fragile — element of this plan. Unlike a Play Store install (2 steps), APK install requires 6–8 user actions including a security warning. Every rupee on Android paid media is wasted if the website is not rebuilt first.

📱 PRE-LAUNCH DEPENDENCY No Android paid media goes live until the APK download page achieves a minimum 15% visit-to-download-click rate. Estimated rebuild: 4–6 weeks, 1 developer + 1 designer.

Funnel Drop-Off at Each Step

1
Ad Click → Website Visit

Target: 100% reach the APK page. Page must load under 3 seconds on 4G.

2
Visit → APK Download Click

Current: ~8%. Target after rebuild: 20–25%. Fix: full page redesign with trust signals, clear CTA, install guide on-page.

3
Download Click → "Enable Unknown Sources" Completed

Estimated drop-off: 35–45%. Biggest loss point. Fix: show step-by-step screenshots for 3 major Android skins (Stock, Samsung One UI, Xiaomi MIUI) BEFORE the user clicks download.

4
Install → App Open

Drop-off: ~15%. Fix: AppsFlyer deferred deep link routes user directly to registration screen on first open — improves registration rate 20–30%.

5
App Open → Registration

Drop-off: ~30%. Offer download bonus only visible to new registrations. WhatsApp OTP reduces friction vs email.

6
Registration → First Ticket Purchase

Drop-off: ~55%. Push notification + WhatsApp reminder within 24 hours. Draw countdown + jackpot amount. First-purchase discount.

Protect360 Fraud Rules

  • Block CTIT <10 seconds — almost certainly click injection fraud
  • Block CTIT >24 hours — suggests click hijacking
  • Block devices with >3 installs in 30 days with same fingerprint but different device IDs
  • Flag any affiliate sub-publisher driving >500 installs/day from a single IP range
  • Hold payment if affiliate drives >80% installs with 0 registrations within 7 days

Section 10
Risks & Mitigation
Five material risks could compromise execution. Ad account bans are the highest probability risk. The APK trust barrier is the highest impact risk. Attribution gaps are manageable through proper AppsFlyer implementation.
🔴 Risk 1: Ad Account Bans (Meta & Google)
Meta and Google can suspend accounts running lottery advertising without proper certification. A ban during peak Phase 2 spend could cost ₹50–150L in lost efficiency and months of rebuilding.
Apply for gambling certification on both platforms in Week 1. Maintain a backup Meta Business Manager account. Keep copies of all creative approvals. Appoint a compliance reviewer. Build a 30-day creative bank in advance so a ban doesn't stop delivery.
🔴 Risk 2: APK Trust Barrier — User Psychology
The "Unknown Sources" security warning is a genuine psychological barrier. Many users have been warned about APK scams. If not pre-empted, conversion rates will be 5–10% instead of 20–25%, making Android acquisition 3–4x more expensive than modelled.
Enforce APK page rebuild as hard pre-launch gate. Deploy WhatsApp-assisted onboarding. Brief influencers to address "is this safe?" proactively. Include Govt of Goa seal prominently. Publish a "Security FAQ" explaining why the app is not on Play Store (policy, not safety).
🟡 Risk 3: Legal / Compliance Risk by State
India's lottery legal framework is a patchwork of state laws. Inadvertently reaching users in banned states could trigger legal action.
Geo-fence ALL paid campaigns — only target legal states. Use IP-based geo-blocking on website. Retain gaming/lottery legal counsel for quarterly compliance review. Monitor any changes in state lottery legislation quarterly.
🟡 Risk 4: Attribution Gaps for APK Installs
Without Google Play referrer, 20–30% of Android installs may be unattributed in early weeks, causing budget misallocation.
Implement AppsFlyer Custom Store tracking and probabilistic attribution in Week 1 — QA before any paid media launches. Accept ~70–80% accuracy vs ~95% for Play Store. Use UTM + AppsFlyer for cross-validation. Don't optimise until 1,000+ installs tracked per channel.
🟡 Risk 5: Budget Burn Without Conversion
Scaling paid media too early (before APK page and creative are optimised) is the most common — and expensive — mistake in app launch marketing.
Enforce Phase Gate reviews requiring leadership sign-off. Set hard budget caps with automated alerts when daily CPT exceeds 2x target. Pause any channel if 7-day rolling CPT exceeds ₹3,500. Early failure indicators: CTR <0.4%, registration rate <20%, D1 retention <30%.

Overall Risk Rating: Medium-High, Manageable. All five risks are mitigable through disciplined process and phased spending. The conservative Phase 1 approach exists precisely to de-risk the larger Phase 2 commitment. A rigorous Phase 1 gate review is the single most important risk management action in this document.
